## Replica Architecture

### Studio Registries
- BrandRegistry: locked logo, typography, colors, image direction, prohibited changes.
- ModelRegistry: model id, name, age, identity, niche, persona, source-pack status, rights status.
- AssetRegistry: image/video/audio/script assets with source, rights, QA, approval, and usage status.
- ContentRegistry: 300-topic model, platform, hook, CTA, funnel stage, approval status.
- MediaJobRegistry: requested generation, tool lane, prompt, negative constraints, owner, QA status.
- CommerceRegistry: Black Card, digital products, licensing inquiries, draft-only Shopify/Stripe readiness.
- AnalyticsRegistry: source, campaign, UTM, metrics, confidence, next optimization.

### Workflow Graph
- BRAND_LOCK_CHECK
- MODEL_SOURCE_PACK_CHECK
- CONTENT_PLAN_GENERATION
- MEDIA_JOB_DRAFT

### Required Gates
- No false-human claims.
- No adult/sexualized styling for younger profiles; strict fashion-only for ages 18-20.
- No public use before asset QA and approval.
- No HeyGen training before explicit approval.
- No Shopify mutation before explicit approval.
- No production deploy before explicit approval.
- No unsupported revenue/follower/performance claims.

### Better-Than-Original Candidate Improvements
- Brand, model, content, media, commerce, and analytics become separate but linked registries.
- Every public asset has rights, disclosure, source, QA, and rollback metadata.
- Black Card/membership and licensing lanes are separated from content production.
- A release firewall decides readiness instead of ad hoc handoffs.
- Validation plan tests happy path, missing approval, unsafe claim, unavailable API, and rollback.
