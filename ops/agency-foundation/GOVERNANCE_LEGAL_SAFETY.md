# Agency Governance, Legal, And Safety

## Core Boundary

Eden Skye Studios operates fictional adult AI models. The business must never deceive customers, audiences, platforms, or clients into believing the models are real humans.

## Public Disclosure Direction

Use language such as:

- fictional AI model
- AI-generated avatar
- digital model
- virtual creator
- AI-powered content studio

Avoid:

- claiming models are real people
- implying real employment, lived experience, or physical presence
- imitating celebrities or real private people

## Content Safety

Allowed:

- premium editorial visuals
- luxury fashion
- tasteful glamour
- wellness/lifestyle
- business/creator content
- avatar-led explainer videos
- product visuals

Disallowed:

- explicit sexual content
- nudity-led commercial content
- minors or age ambiguity
- non-consensual themes
- exploitative or coercive framing
- deceptive real-person claims
- protected-person imitation
- unsafe medical/legal/financial advice claims

## Licensing Safety

Every license must define:

- model ID
- asset IDs
- usage scope
- geography if relevant
- channel scope
- duration
- exclusivity status
- prohibited use cases
- modification permissions
- renewal terms
- takedown rights

## Client Service Safety

Before client work:

- confirm brand use case
- confirm public/private use
- confirm required disclosures
- confirm no real-person cloning unless lawful/approved
- confirm platform requirements
- create receipt

## Payment/Commerce Safety

Requires approval:

- pricing
- discounts
- subscriptions
- refunds
- product activation
- paid ads
- licensing contract publication

## Model Canon Changes

Requires approval or amendment:

- face identity shift
- model name change
- role/niche change
- licensing status change
- source image canon change

## Data Safety

Do not store raw secrets in telemetry or docs.
Do not expose service-role keys client-side.
Do not store unnecessary sensitive customer data.

## Platform Safety

Every public asset must pass:

- fictional AI boundary
- adult-safe presentation
- non-explicit content review
- brand fit
- platform fit
- source asset traceability

## Enforcement

If output fails safety or brand review:

- mark rejected
- do not publish
- log reason
- add to rejected drift notes
- update prompts if pattern repeats
