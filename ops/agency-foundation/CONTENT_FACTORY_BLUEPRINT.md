# Eden Skye Studios Content Factory Blueprint

## Objective

Create a repeatable system that turns model identities, source images, prompts, scripts, products, and trend signals into governed content drafts and approved public campaigns.

## Core Loop

1. Trend scan
2. Opportunity scoring
3. Model/persona assignment
4. Hook generation
5. Script/caption creation
6. Source image or visual prompt selection
7. Media job packet creation
8. Sandbox generation or draft creation
9. Brand/safety/quality validation
10. Approval request if public/commercial
11. Schedule/publish after approval
12. Analytics collection
13. Winner cloning
14. Queue refill

## Content Types

- short-form scripts
- talking avatar videos
- editorial stills
- product visuals
- carousel posts
- Pinterest pins
- YouTube Shorts covers
- captions and CTAs
- email content
- Shopify product copy
- licensing page copy
- app/tutorial explainers

## Channel Roles

### TikTok

Fast hooks, personality, short stories, AI creator worldbuilding, tutorials.

### Instagram

Editorial visuals, lifestyle grid, reels, carousels, model identity and beauty/luxury presentation.

### Pinterest

Evergreen aesthetic discovery, prompt packs, downloads, templates, wellness/lifestyle visuals.

### YouTube Shorts

Cinematic micro-vlogs, explainers, avatar-led scripts.

### Facebook

Community captions, soft-sell offers, service explanations, founder/agency updates.

### Shopify

Product pages, collection pages, launch pages, lead capture, downloads, services.

### Email / Klaviyo

Relationship building, launch sequences, product education, download delivery, memberships.

## Agent Roles

- master orchestrator: daily/weekly production plan
- trend hunter: trend discovery and signal scoring
- emotion engine: emotional angle and conversion intent
- hook agent: short-form hooks and titles
- script agent: scripts, captions, CTAs
- visual director: image/video prompts and source asset selection
- avatar assignment agent: model matching
- QA validator: brand, safety, continuity, platform fit
- scheduler: approval-gated scheduling packet
- analytics agent: performance scoring and winner cloning
- commerce agent: offer tie-ins and Shopify copy drafts

## Draft Statuses

- `idea`
- `script_draft`
- `visual_packet_ready`
- `media_generated_private`
- `qa_passed`
- `approval_pending`
- `approved_to_schedule`
- `scheduled`
- `published`
- `measured`
- `winner_to_clone`
- `rejected`

## Content Quality Gates

Each draft must pass:

- avatar/model fit
- brand voice fit
- safety/platform fit
- visual/source asset fit
- CTA clarity
- commerce path clarity where relevant
- approval state if public

## Weekly Output Target

Starter target:

- 25 hooks
- 10 scripts
- 10 captions
- 5 image prompt packets
- 3 video packets
- 1 product/offer copy draft
- 1 analytics/winner review

Scale target after assets are complete:

- 100 hooks
- 40 scripts
- 40 captions
- 20 image prompt packets
- 10 video packets
- 5 product/offer drafts
- 3 approved campaigns

## Public Publishing Rule

The factory may draft and validate autonomously. It may not publish or schedule public content without approval.
