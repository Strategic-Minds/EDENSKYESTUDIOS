## Licensing Products

### Image License

Licensed use of approved fictional AI model images.

Tiers:

- personal/internal use
- creator/social use
- commercial campaign use
- extended brand use
- exclusive campaign use

### Video License

Licensed use of approved model-led video assets.

Tiers:

- internal training/demo
- social organic
- paid campaign
- product/landing page
- exclusive brand campaign

### Model Persona License

Limited use of a fictional model persona, name, and visual direction for approved campaigns.

Requires:

- model ID
- usage window
- channel scope
- approval receipt
- prohibited uses
- renewal/expiration
