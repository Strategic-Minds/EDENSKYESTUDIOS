# Eden Skye Studios Master Agency Blueprint

## Company Definition

Eden Skye Studios is a fictional-AI digital modeling agency, content studio, and creator-commerce company.

The company builds and operates a roster of fictional adult AI models who can be used for:

- branded image production
- avatar videos
- social media content
- digital campaigns
- licensing packages
- product and service marketing
- downloads and templates
- creator tools and apps
- Shopify storefront offers
- client-facing content services

## Strategic Positioning

Eden Skye Studios is positioned as a premium, controlled, brand-safe AI modeling and media production agency.

Core promise:

> Beautiful, consistent, governed AI models and content systems for premium brands, creators, commerce, and digital products.

## Business Pillars

### 1. AI Model Roster

A controlled stable of fictional adult AI models with IDs, visual canon, voice direction, content lanes, source images, and licensing rules.

### 2. Content Creation Studio

Scripts, stills, reels, product visuals, social campaigns, storyboards, short-form videos, ads, and content calendars.

### 3. Avatar Video Production

HeyGen-style talking avatar videos, campaign clips, educational explainers, brand messages, and model-led content.

### 4. Digital Products And Downloads

Prompt packs, brand kits, caption packs, content calendars, avatar system templates, creator workflow kits, and image prompt vaults.

### 5. Services

Done-for-you AI avatar content setup, brand avatar packages, social content production, Shopify launch support, content audits, and automation setup.

### 6. Licensing

Licensed use of approved fictional AI models, images, clips, prompts, templates, and brand-safe campaign assets under defined usage tiers.

### 7. Apps And Tools

Future lightweight apps for prompt generation, avatar management, campaign planning, content calendars, approval queues, media asset tracking, and client portals.

## Governance Doctrine

Eden Skye Studios can operate with high autonomy only inside sandbox, draft, and receipt-logged lanes. Anything live, public, financial, schema-level, credential-level, or production-level requires Jeremy's explicit approval.
