# Signal Intake Specification

## Purpose
Define the intake surfaces and signal types the agent should process automatically.

## Signal classes
- trend signals
- keyword signals
- phrase signals
- creator signals
- competitor signals
- audience signals
- performance signals
- visual signals
- market and policy signals
- product and offer signals

## Intake priority
1. direct user-provided source material
2. verified project docs and registries
3. public trend and platform research
4. historical memory and prior winners

## Preferred outputs from intake
- ranked opportunities
- anomalies worth investigating
- repeated audience pain points
- content hooks worth testing
- narratives gaining momentum
- persona-platform matches

## Noise filters
Deprioritize:
- weak vanity spikes with no audience fit
- outrage bait without conversion value
- duplicate trend chatter with no whitespace
- signals that conflict with persona boundaries
