# Xyla Content Feed Standard

## Purpose
Define the content packet standard Eden Skye Studios uses to feed Xyla-ready image, video, caption, CTA, and platform instructions.

## Brand Lock
Use the approved ES monogram and Eden Skye Studios wordmark. Do not redesign the logo, substitute generic Shopify typography, or publish unapproved identity variants.

## Default Status
All packets are draft-only until approved.

## Packet Fields
- packet_id
- source_page
- avatar_id
- avatar_name
- category
- platform_targets
- visual_prompt
- video_prompt
- caption
- CTA
- hashtag_family
- keywords
- product_or_offer_link
- approval_status
- publishing_enabled
- metricool_enabled
- xyla_generation_enabled

## Rules
- publishing_enabled must default to false.
- metricool_enabled must default to false.
- xyla_generation_enabled must default to false unless a human gate approves generation.
- No live posting or scheduling is authorized by this document.
- All outputs must pass brand, PAOS, rights, and platform review.

## Daily Feed Pattern
Post 1: beauty, model, emotion, cinematic identity.
Post 2: education, worldbuilding, AI modeling agency value.
Post 3: monetization, Shopify, licensing, download, Black Card, or service CTA.

## Human Gate
Required before image/video generation, public posting, scheduling, Shopify product binding, paid ads, or licensing use.
