## Executive Summary

This plan defines the financial operating system for Eden Skye Studios. It does not present verified accounting, tax, legal, or investment advice. It defines how the business should track revenue, costs, margin, runway, thresholds, and decision rules without inventing financial truth.

## Financial Source Rules

1. Verified actuals come only from source systems: Shopify, Stripe, bank/accounting exports, vendor invoices, Vercel, Supabase, and approved spreadsheets.
2. Planning assumptions must be labeled `Assumption`.
3. Forecasts must be labeled `Forecast`.
4. No agent may claim profitability, runway, CAC, or revenue traction without verified data.

## KPI Stack

- Revenue: gross revenue, net revenue, revenue by offer/channel, AOV, repeat purchase, MRR, premium access revenue, licensing/service revenue.
- Margin: gross margin, contribution margin, fulfillment labor, AI/media cost, platform fees, payment fees, refunds and chargebacks.
- Growth: landing conversion, product conversion, lead-to-purchase, waitlist opt-in, content-to-sale, paid CAC when approved.
- Operations: queue age, blocker count, failed automations, manual steps eliminated, documentation drift count.

## Decision Rules

- Do not add recurring tools unless tied to a revenue path, compliance requirement, or measurable time savings.
- Do not scale ad spend until the landing page, offer, tracking, and fulfillment path are validated.
- Do not discount without margin logic.
- Do not launch a high-touch service without scope, minimum price, and delivery checklist.
- Do not change live pricing, payments, subscriptions, discounts, refunds, or payouts without explicit approval.

## Trigger Types

| Trigger | Required action |
|---|---|
| Margin below target | Pause scaling and review pricing, cost, or fulfillment. |
| Conversion below target | Diagnose messaging, offer, page, audience, or traffic quality. |
| CAC above payback limit | Stop paid spend and review acquisition strategy. |
| Refund/chargeback spike | Pause promotion and review quality, claims, fulfillment, and policy. |
| Documentation drift | Run the sync policy and update Drive/repo mirrors. |

## Required Artifacts

- KPI dashboard or spreadsheet.
- Monthly forecast model.
- Offer margin table.
- Tool and vendor cost register.
- Experiment budget log.
- Approval log for protected pricing, payment, spend, and production decisions.
