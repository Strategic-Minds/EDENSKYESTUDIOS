## Executive Summary

Eden Skye Studios should grow as a premium AI creator commerce brand with a disciplined content-to-commerce engine. Growth should come from validated offers, not random content volume or uncontrolled automation.

## Positioning

- Fictional AI/virtual creator identity.
- Premium, cinematic, emotionally intelligent visual brand.
- Shopify-led public commerce plus Vercel-led private/member experience.
- Productized digital assets, memberships, premium access, licensing, and creator-service packages.
- Platform-safe sensual luxury positioning without explicit or deceptive claims.

## Growth Loops

1. Content to product: turn high-signal visuals and hooks into downloads, product drops, or memberships.
2. Product to membership: use entry purchases and repeat interest to drive membership, Black Card, and Edens Closet access.
3. Creator assets to B2B: package proven workflows into licensing, avatar/video packs, and creator automation services.
4. Operations to automation: automate repeated manual work only after the workflow is understood and validated.

## Go-To-Market Lanes

| Lane | First proof signal | Scale condition |
|---|---|---|
| Downloads | Purchase, add-to-cart, waitlist | Repeat purchases and acceptable margin |
| Membership | Upgrade intent or paid subscription | Retention and support load are viable |
| Black Card | Qualified inquiry or purchase | High perceived value and gated experience are clear |
| Licensing | Paid pilot or signed scope | Usage rights and production workflow are clear |
| Creator services | Discovery call or paid audit | Delivery checklist and margin are validated |

## Content Pillars

- Eden Skye visual world.
- Edens Closet private experience.
- Product drops and digital assets.
- Creator workflow and automation education.
- Trust, governance, fictional AI identity, and safe use.

## Growth Rules

- Every content lane should point to a measurable next action.
- Engagement is not revenue proof.
- No public publishing, live pricing change, paid spend, or Shopify mutation without approval.
- Scale only after conversion, margin, support burden, and brand risk are reviewed.
