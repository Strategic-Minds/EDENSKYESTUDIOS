## Executive Summary

Eden Skye Studios is a fictional AI luxury creator brand and commerce system built around premium digital identity, memberships, downloads, licenses, creator-service products, and private member experiences such as Edens Closet.

The operating recommendation is to run Eden Skye Studios as a governed premium AI media and commerce brand with three connected revenue lanes:

1. Consumer and fan commerce through Shopify.
2. Member/private experience access through Edens Closet and the Vercel app.
3. Higher-ticket B2B or creator services such as image packs, content systems, avatar/video production packages, licensing, and brand partnership assets.

## Operating Model

| Layer | Owner | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Public commerce | Shopify | Products, memberships, downloads, licenses, services, checkout. |
| Private app/control | Vercel via EDENSKYESTUDIOS | Login, Edens Closet, admin, chat, previews, bridge APIs. |
| Governance/control plane | AUTO_BUILDER | Recursive planning, validation, bridge routing, approval gates. |
| Human-facing canon | Drive `EDEN_SKYE_STUDIOS_OS` | Strategy, finance, receipts, release gates, source truth. |

## Offer Stack

- Entry: digital downloads, stock image/model assets, starter content packs.
- Core: memberships, Black Card/premium access, Edens Closet, AI chat/guided experiences.
- Expansion: avatar/video packs, licensing packages, brand partnerships, creator automation services.

## Growth Stages

1. Canon alignment and offer readiness.
2. Storefront and private experience preview.
3. Conversion validation.
4. Automation and fulfillment hardening.
5. Scale and partnership expansion.

## Governance Rules

- Keep Eden Skye clearly fictional/AI where identity clarity matters.
- Keep Shopify public commerce separate from Vercel private/control surfaces.
- Treat all financial numbers as assumptions until verified from source systems.
- No production deploy, Shopify mutation, public publishing, payment/pricing change, live avatar/video action, Supabase production write, destructive Drive move, or destructive GitHub action without explicit approval.

## Immediate Priorities

1. Keep the canon stack synchronized in Drive and both repos.
2. Validate Shopify-to-Vercel buyer/member journey.
3. Validate downloads, membership, and premium access before scaling.
4. Attach every automation upgrade to revenue, quality, speed, margin, or risk reduction.
