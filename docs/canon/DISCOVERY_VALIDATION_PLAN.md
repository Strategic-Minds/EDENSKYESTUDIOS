## Executive Summary

Eden Skye Studios should learn before scaling. The recursive governance agent must run disciplined discovery loops that identify demand, validate offers, capture feedback, and update canon without inventing results or bypassing approval gates.

## Discovery Lanes

| Lane | Purpose | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Audience research | Understand buyer motivations and objections | Audience insight brief |
| Offer research | Learn what people will pay for | Offer proof score |
| Benchmark research | Compare positioning, pricing, product mix, and channels | Benchmark memo |
| Channel research | Learn where attention converts | Channel scorecard |
| Brand-safety research | Protect platform and trust boundaries | Risk flags and claim rules |
| Workflow research | Find operational friction | Automation opportunity list |

## Validation Loops

1. Offer validation: landing pages, waitlists, product previews, downloads, memberships, premium access inquiry, or paid pilot.
2. Messaging validation: headline, promise, CTA, and product-positioning tests.
3. Channel validation: organic content, visual discovery, email/SMS when approved, partnership outreach.
4. Member experience validation: repeat logins, upgrade interest, private experience usage, feedback.
5. Automation validation: time saved, fewer blockers, fewer manual steps, better documentation freshness.

## Proof Scoring

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 0 | No evidence or could not verify |
| 1 | Weak qualitative interest |
| 2 | Engagement but no buyer intent |
| 3 | Buyer intent such as opt-in, checkout start, or call request |
| 4 | Paid validation or qualified pipeline |
| 5 | Repeatable paid validation with acceptable margin and low risk |

Only scores 4-5 should be considered scale candidates.

## Agent Rules

Agents may draft experiment briefs, score tests, summarize evidence, recommend next tests, update validation docs, and queue approval requests. Agents may not run paid ads, public posts, Shopify mutations, pricing changes, or production deploys without approval.

## Immediate Validation Priorities

1. Confirm the Shopify-to-Vercel buyer/member journey.
2. Validate downloads, membership, and premium access.
3. Define tracking before public launch.
4. Create weekly validation reports.
5. Tie every automation upgrade to speed, quality, revenue, margin, or risk reduction.
