# EDEN SKYE OS Discovery Engine

## Status
SCAFFOLD_ONLY / ADDITIVE SOURCE ARTIFACT.

## Purpose
The Discovery Engine identifies content opportunities, source materials, market signals, competitor patterns, product angles, audience needs, and reusable campaign concepts for Eden Skye Studios.

## Inputs
- Brand canon
- Approved offers
- Shopify product/catalog data
- Metricool analytics
- Social platform trends
- Search/SEO data
- User-approved briefs
- Prior content performance

## Outputs
- Discovery packets
- Opportunity scores
- Content angles
- Audience hypotheses
- Source references
- Generator-ready briefs

## Required Gates
- Source truth check
- Brand fit check
- Claim safety check
- Monetization alignment check
- Human approval before publication

## Runtime Contract
Default runtime mode is read-only discovery. The engine may draft opportunities but must not publish, schedule, spend credits, mutate Shopify, change billing, deploy code, or alter Supabase schema without explicit approval.

## Completion Criteria
Discovery Engine is complete when it can produce ranked, source-backed content opportunities with clear evidence, confidence, and next-action routing.
